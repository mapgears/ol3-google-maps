Watch options example

Demonstrates the watch options

You can choose to let OL3 render the layers over the Google Maps basemap. It will cause a visible lag when panning or zooming, but it will eliminate any limitation of the library for that type of layer.

In this example, you can enable and disable watching different types of layers with the buttons below. When it's green, it means Google Maps is allowed to render it when a Google Layer is visible. When it's red, OpenLayers renders that layer type at all times.
